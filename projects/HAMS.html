    • Project HAMS

    Project High Altitude MultiStage (HAMS) Goal is to design a multi stage high altitude X-Plane. Final design should consist of a launch vehicle to ~1,000 ft then a rocket powered X-Plane to ~4,000ft. Numbers are subject to change dependent on experiments.

    Sub-Project Sisyphus L-1: Meteor design specs:
    • 400ft. Service Ceiling
    • Wood with plastic/fabric wrapping
    • 4lb payload externally mounted
    • 3.5mm bullet drive (or similar)
    • LiPo power store
    • Parachute or Skid landing
    • 1m wingspan
    Sub-Project Bullet
    • M-1: Comet design specs
    • C6-5 rocket engine
    • Capable of mid air control
    • 90% reliability of ignition
